Professional Overview

With over nineteen years of experience working as a producer, digital project manager, and Product Manager, I have developed an extensive skillset in managing digital advertising teams and international development projects. Throughout my career, I have donned several hats and can confidently call myself a digital native and thought leader in this space.

Currently delivering freelance programme and project management across AI, digital, and experiential projects for international organisations including the Inter-American Development Bank (IDB) and innovative tech startups. I specialise in GDPR-compliant AI-driven solutions, strategic outreach campaigns, and scalable technology roadmaps.

I excel in managing the day-to-day production flow for digital advertising teams, from grouping resources for internal production to assembling advertising proposals and managing outsourcing. My experience also includes art directing green screened video shoots, structuring timings and scoping budgets/financials for pre-production documentation.

I have had the privilege of working with some of the world's most prestigious and leading brands across different sectors and industries. My experience has allowed me to work in fast-paced environments, handling multiple projects and managing cross-functional, offshore/3rd party teams using waterfall and agile methodologies. I have also worked with CEOs and executives of companies (UK, Europe, USA, and Caribbean), implementing new technologies on websites AI (CMS & DAM) and managing accounts.

I have a solid understanding of content management systems and can translate technical terminology for stakeholders in non-technical roles. Possessing valuable strategic product and project management skills, I excel in backlog refinement, writing user stories, and keeping within project budgetary requirements and deadlines. I have managed budgets of up to $3.4 million and have a detail-oriented and thorough approach to defining requirements and outlining specifications.

I am a creative multi-skilled and technically minded individual in most areas of digital advertising, with a good planner and problem-solver mindset. My in-depth understanding of Artificial intelligence, cryptocurrency, Web 3, and digital products allows me to make informed decisions while managing projects.
